Bonnie:

Model: Rabbit
Main color: Indigo
Accent color: Blue
Eye color: Pink

Bonnie is an animatronic rabbit and a children's entertainer housed at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, alongside Freddy Fazbear and Chica. Bonnie serves as the guitarist in Freddy's band and is positioned on the left side of the stage.

    Appearence:

  • Bonnie is an indigo-colored animatronic rabbit with blue accents and magenta eyes.
  • He has a squarish muzzle and long articulated ears that can bend backwards and forwards.
  • Bonnie has round teeth and lacks eyebrows, unlike the other characters
*While shown as purple is most artworks, he's mostly shown as dark blue in games.

    personality & Functionality:

  • Bonnie performs on stage as a back-up singer and guitarist for the animatronic band
  • Not being fond of adults, Bonnie is a vengeful spirit and has a sadistic behavior towards them at night
  • Most active at night and is first the leave the stage but with some unfair tactics
  • Even with persistance, he tends to give up quickly and exhibits impatience that is indicated with twitching and goraing sounds
  • Likes to toy with the night guard and stares into the cameras as intimdation
  • It is implied that he hates cramped spaces as he is known the damage cameras in Pirate cover out of sheer frustration of having to share a space with Foxy
  • Bonnie greatly enjoys playing his guitar and is very prideful about it
  • Bonnie to have a personality based on a rock musician with the speech pattern of a laid back hippie stereotype.

    In-game Behaviors:

  1. Five Nights at Freddy's 1:
    • Bonnie becomes active on Night 1 and becomes increasingly aggressive.
    • He follows a set path from the Show Stage to the Dining Area, West Hall, and finally the Office to attack.
    • The night guard must closely monitor Bonnie's location using security cameras and close the doors in time to prevent his entry.

  2. Ultimate Custom Night:

    • Bonnie is a selectable animatronic, allowing players to customize his behavior and difficulty.
    • Players must strategically manage Bonnie's movements, monitor his position, and use tools to survive and defend against him.

  3. Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted:

    • Bonnie retains his classic behaviors from the original game in the virtual reality version.
    • He moves through the building and tries to reach the player's location.
    • Failure to track Bonnie's movements and defend against him results in a jump scare and game over.

  4. Five Nights at Freddy's AR: Special Delivery:

    • In the augmented reality game, Bonnie becomes an active threat in the player's real-world environment.
    • Using GPS and augmented reality technology, Bonnie stalks and hunts the player unexpectedly.
    • Players must defend themselves using tools and tactics to avoid Bonnie's scare and attack.
      • Bonnie's appearance and unpredictable behavior contribute to the suspense and challenge of the Five Nights at Freddy's series, keeping players on edge as they strive to survive each night.
